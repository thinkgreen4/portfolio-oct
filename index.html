About Me

I am 27 years old and was born and raised in Salt Lake. I love the outdoors and have been an avid skier since I was three. My hobbies include skiing, music, swimming,camping, and being outdoors. I feel extremely peaceful and calm whenever I am in nature. I broke my spine when I was 13 and am paralyzed from the waist down. I still try and enjoy all of my favorite things. Some of my favorite past times are Mono-skiing and Biking. My injury has taught me a ton and really shaped who I am as a person.

I graduated from the University of Utah in 20016 with a bachelor degree in Economics. The global economy and it's history have always fascinated me. I got into coding through online courses on my own and recently enrolled in a bootcamp to polish my skills. One driving force was seeing how important the tech industry has been to globalization and trade. This inspired me to pursue a career. Other factor driving my decision was the last collapse of the financial sector, and the security in the technology pushed me to continue my education into coding.

The creativity and problem solving required to build websites is what keeps me interested. I know I still have a lot to learn but really enjoy doing this. I am looking for an internship or a junior developer position. The pay is not a factor and I am more interested in getting experience and my foot in the door. I would rather be part of a project that teaches me than getting payed well doing basic repetitive tasks.
